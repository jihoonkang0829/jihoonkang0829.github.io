With the emergence of the novel coronavirus, our society has witnessed soar in racial discrimination and hate crime since last year. We propose a model to predict occurences of hate crimes to contribute to the efficient prevention of the hate crimes.
We first collect racial discrimination-related event instances with New York Times API, especially the date, location and the respective occurences of the crimes.
Based on the data collected, we train a deep learning model to predict occurences and locations of future possible discrimanation-related events.
We create a recurrent model that predicts the following week's hate crime occurences in state level, based on the previous 4 weeks' occurences by states.
